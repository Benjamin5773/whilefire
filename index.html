About WhileFire
What is WhileFire
Whilefire is an app which could be used during wildfires. It is
targeted towards individuals living in wildfire-prone areas and
professionals who may encounter wildfires, such as forestry
managers. The app allows users to report wildfires promptly,
mitigate risks, and stay in touch with family and friends.
